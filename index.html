Three Letters, Kept for You

01

To Daakshii —

Every love story has a chapter where two people almost stop writing it. Ours had one too. But I never stopped thinking about the version of us that gets to grow old, silly, and soft together. That's the version I'm choosing, every single day from here.

02

To Daakshii —

Daakshii, 🤍 Pehle mujhe lagta tha ki "I love you" keh dena hi pyaar hota hai. Lekin tumne mujhe sikhaya ki pyaar sirf words nahi hota. Pyaar respect hota hai. Patience hota hai. Trust hota hai. Aur sabse zyada... actions hote hain. Main maanta hoon ki main wahi actions hamesha nahi dikha paaya jinke tum haqdar thi. Aur us baat ka mujhe sach me afsos hai. Ab main tumse promises nahi karna chahta. Main bas itna chahta hoon ki ek din tum khud meri efforts dekhkar keh sako... "Haan... ye wahi Aryan hai jo sach me badal gaya." Us din se zyada khoobsurat mere liye kuch nahi hoga. 🌹

03

To Daakshii —

Thank you for the chance you didn't have to give me. I know trust, once shaken, has to be rebuilt one small honest moment at a time — so that's what I'm here for. Not one grand gesture. A thousand small, unglamorous, faithful ones.
